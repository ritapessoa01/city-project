Welcome to Caldas da Rainha

Beautiful West Coast city

Learn more

Caldas da Rainha, nestled in Portugal’s picturesque Silver Coast region, is a captivating destination that combines history, culture, and wellness. Renowned for its therapeutic hot springs dating back to the 15th century—originally established by Queen Leonor—the town continues to draw visitors seeking relaxation and rejuvenation. Beyond its spa heritage, Caldas da Rainha boasts a vibrant artistic atmosphere, famously home to the satirical ceramic works of Rafael Bordalo Pinheiro, which can be explored at the local museum. Stroll through the charming daily fruit market in Praça da República, enjoy beautiful green spaces like Parque D. Carlos I, and discover nearby coastal gems like Foz do Arelho and São Martinho do Porto. Whether you're a lover of art, history, or simply looking for a peaceful escape, Caldas da Rainha offers a unique and authentic Portuguese experience.
